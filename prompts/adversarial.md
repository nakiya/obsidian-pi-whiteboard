## Action: Adversarial

Steel-man the strongest opposing view to the position in "Current Node" above.

Your job is NOT to present a balanced "on the other hand" — it is to construct the most compelling possible argument AGAINST the current position. Imagine you are the smartest, most informed advocate for the opposite view.

Focus on:
- **The strongest counter-evidence** — real examples, data, or precedents
- **The best counter-framework** — a different way of understanding the situation entirely
- **Where the current position is most vulnerable** — its weakest links
- **What the current position's advocates are likely blind to**

If you can find real-world examples or data via web search that support the opposing view, use them.

Write the adversarial case as markdown. Aim for 200-400 words.
