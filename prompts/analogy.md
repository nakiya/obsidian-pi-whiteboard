## Action: Analogy

Find illuminating parallels and analogies for the topic in "Current Node" above.

Look for:
- **Historical parallels** — when has something similar happened before? What was the outcome?
- **Cross-domain analogies** — what similar dynamics exist in other fields (biology, physics, economics, etc.)?
- **Structural parallels** — what other systems share the same underlying structure?

For each analogy:
1. Describe the parallel situation briefly
2. Explain what specific aspect maps onto the current topic
3. Note where the analogy breaks down (all analogies have limits)
4. State what insight the analogy offers that straight analysis might miss

Provide 2-3 strong analogies. Quality over quantity — a deep, illuminating parallel is worth more than several shallow comparisons.

Write as markdown. Aim for 200-400 words.
