## Action: Critique

Critically analyze the claim or topic in "Current Node" above.

Focus on:
- **Weak assumptions** — what is being taken for granted that might not hold?
- **Missing context** — what important factors are being ignored?
- **Logical gaps** — where does the reasoning break down?
- **Alternative explanations** — what else could explain the same observations?
- **Scale and magnitude** — is the claimed effect actually significant?

Be genuinely critical, not diplomatically balanced. If the claim is strong, say so and explain why. If it's weak, say that clearly.

Write your critique as markdown. Aim for 200-400 words.
