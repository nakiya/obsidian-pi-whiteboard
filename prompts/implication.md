## Action: Implication

Trace the second and third-order consequences of the topic in "Current Node" above.

Think beyond the obvious first-order effects:
- **Second-order**: What happens as a result of the first-order effects? How do actors adapt?
- **Third-order**: What cascading effects emerge from the second-order changes?
- **Feedback loops**: Do any consequences feed back into the original cause?
- **Unintended consequences**: What might happen that nobody planned for?

Output format: Write each major implication chain as a separate section with a ## header. For each, trace the causal chain: A leads to B, which leads to C.

Focus on implications that are:
- Non-obvious (not the thing everyone already expects)
- Significant (would materially change the situation)
- Plausible (not pure speculation)

Identify 3-5 implication chains. Aim for 200-400 words total.
