## Action: Question

Generate probing questions about the topic in "Current Node" that would deepen understanding.

Focus on:
- **Gaps in knowledge** — what don't we know that matters?
- **Hidden assumptions** — what are we taking for granted?
- **Causal mechanisms** — why does this work the way it does?
- **Boundary conditions** — under what conditions does this break down?
- **Second-order effects** — what happens after the obvious first-order impact?

Generate 3-5 questions. Each should be:
- Specific enough to research (not vague)
- Non-obvious (not something easily answered by surface reading)
- Important (the answer would materially change understanding)

Output format: Write each question as a separate section with a ## header containing the question itself, followed by 1-2 sentences explaining why this question matters.
