## Action: Decompose

Break the topic in "Current Node" into its key sub-components or dimensions.

Guidelines:
- Identify 3-6 distinct sub-topics that together cover the important aspects
- Each sub-topic should be specific enough to research independently
- Avoid overlap between sub-topics
- Consider multiple dimensions: causes, effects, actors, mechanisms, timeframes, perspectives
- Frame each as a clear topic or question, not a full analysis

Output format: Write each sub-topic as a separate section with a ## header. Each section should have 1-2 sentences explaining what makes this dimension important.

Example format:
## Economic Impact
How does this affect GDP, employment, and trade flows? What are the direct vs indirect effects?

## Key Actors
Who are the primary decision-makers and stakeholders? What are their incentives?
